$$

$$  
\frac{d(Dependent)}{d(InDependent)} + P_{(InDependent)}(Dependent) = Q_{(InDependent)}  
$$
  
 **Step 1 ‚Äî Identify**  
$$  
P_{(InDependent)}, \quad Q_{(InDependent)}
$$  
  
 **Step 2 ‚Äî Integrating Factor (IF)**  
$$  
IF = e^{\int P_{(InDependent)}\,d(Independent)}  
$$  

  
 **Step 3 ‚Äî General solution**  
$$
